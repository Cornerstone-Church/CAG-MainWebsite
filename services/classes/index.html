Wednesday Night Classes

January 29th - March 11th, 2020

  • Discover Class

    Fireside Room

    Learn about the DNA, mission & beliefs of Cornerstone. This class is especially for those interested in becoming an official member—someone committed to faithful giving, growing & serving.

  • Exploring My Bible

    Sanctuary

    An overview of the Old and New Testament exploring the major characters, themes, and places of the Bible. A helpful timeline of major events will also be shared.

  • Buddhism: The Darkness of Enlightenment

    Room 101

    Buddhism is an ancient spiritual belief that blinds people to the saving knowledge of Jesus Christ. Come and learn about the history of Buddhism, its beliefs, its presence around the world and its growth in the West. More importantly, learn how we can reach those lost in the darkness of "enlightenment".

  • Deaf Ministry: Second Kings

    Room 102

    Come take a journey through the book of Second Kings and explore some of the miraculous events that occurred testifying to God’s continuing work among His people. Learn about the different kings reigning in both Israel and Judah as we explore almost 300 years of different monarchies. This class is facilitated in sign language.

  • Romans

    Room 103

    This study focuses on Paul’s systematic explanation of the gospel to the Romans and offers insight into the doctrines of sin, salvation, and sanctification. The student will learn how union with Christ and the indwelling Holy Spirit leads to spiritual growth.

  • Managing Conflict

    Room 105

    Got Conflict? In your marriage, family, or work? Join Dr. Ron Sommers for practical insights on managing conflict from Jesus, the Bible, and Dr. Sommers book on Managing Conflict. Study conflict’s origins, purposes, and methods for managing it for God’s glory. Remember: Conflict is normal, combat is not.

  • How to Study the Bible

    Room 108

    Have you ever wanted to improve your Bible study skills? Join us for an interactive class to help with your study time. Learn some great skills to further enhance your quiet time and get the most out of the Word.

  • Doulos Deep Dive

    Room 110

    Are you 18-35? Join the Doulos community as we dive into God’s Word and apply it to our lives in practical ways. We want the Bible to truly be a lamp unto our feet!
