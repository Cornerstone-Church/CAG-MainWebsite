Wednesday Night Classes

  • Doulos Deep Dive

    Room 110

    Are you 18-35? Join the Doulos community as we dive into the God’s Word and apply it to our lives in practical ways. We want the Bible to truly be a lamp unto our feet!

  • The Intentional Remnant

    Sanctuary

    This is a 12-week study intended to motivate three things: Awareness of our role as God's Remnant, United Prayer, and Intentional Godly Living. Join us as we explore Ezekiel's vision, Isaiah's call, and specific ways we can live godly, intentional lives in these dark days; taking it all to God's Throne Room weekly.

  • Rios de Limpieza

    Room 108

    Aprenda a recibir sanidad y liberación de su pasado para que pueda caminar en plenitud de Cristo y conocer la libertad que Él brinda. Donde quiera que esté en su camino, esta clase le puede ayudar a vivir su vida al máximo en Cristo. Temas incluyen: Conocer al Padre, Hablando Palabras de Vida, y otros temas más. La clase culmina con un retiro opcional de un día en un lugar cercano.

  • Cleansing Stream University

    Room 105

    Learn how to receive healing and deliverance from your past, so you can walk in the wholeness of Christ and know the freedom He gives. Wherever you are on your journey, this course can help you live your life in Christ to the fullest. Topics include: Knowing the Father, Speaking Words of Life & more. The class culminates in a one-day optional nearby retreat.

  • Marriage Enrichment: Doing it God’s Way

    Fireside Room

    For married couples or those who are thinking about marriage. Understand marriage as a covenant commitment first to God, then to one another. “Though one may be overpowered, two can defend themselves. A cord of three strands is not quickly broken.”- Ecclesiastes 4:12 (NIV)

  • How to Study the Bible

    Room 101

    Have you ever wanted to improve you Bible study skills? Join us for an interactive class to help with your study time. Learn some great skills to further enhance your quiet time and get the most out of your word.

  • Finding Your Fit in Ministry

    Room 103

    This course will explore your “fit” and “preparedness” for your calling to minister. We will cover spiritual gifts assessment, internships, and specialized training for your equipping for service.

  • Pathfinders: Beyond Basics

    Choir Room

    This six week study will go over and compare the six elements of Special Operations Missions and comparing and applying these elements to our Spiritual Mission that Christ has given us. The study will include looking at purpose, simplicity, speed, security, repetition, and surprise. All Six of these elements give a powerful and strong foundation in our spiritual walks and pursuing the completion of the mission that Christ has given us as believers.

  • Holy Spirit and Water Baptism (Deaf Ministry)

    Room 102

    This class will explore water baptism, why we believe in it, and why it is necessary. The class will also explore the Holy Spirit baptism and associated gifts of the Spirit. This class is facilitated in sign language.
