Wednesday Night Classes

  • Discover Class

    Fireside Room

    Learn about the DNA, mission & beliefs of Cornerstone. This class is especially for those interested in becoming an official member—someone committed to faithful giving, growing & serving.

  • Major Doctrines of the Bible

    Sanctuary

    Join us as we explore the basic doctrines of the bible like the Trinity, The Deity/Humanity of Jesus, Divine Healing, The Inspiration of Scripture, End Time events and more. We will discuss a total of 16 major doctrines.

  • Romans

    Room 105

    This study focuses on Paul’s systematic explanation of the gospel to the Romans and offers insight into the doctrines of sin, salvation, and sanctification. The student will learn how union with Christ and the indwelling Holy Spirit leads to spiritual growth.

  • Doulos Deep Dive

    Room 110

    Are you 18-35? Join the Doulos community as we dive into the God’s Word and apply it to our lives in practical ways. We want the Bible to truly be a lamp unto our feet!

  • Deaf Ministry - Proverbs: Perls of Wisdom

    Room 102

    This in-depth course will study verse-by-verse the book of Proverbs, uncovering it's wisdom for daily living that glorifies God. This class is facilitated in sign language.

  • Financial Peace

    Choir Room

    Understand God's ways of handling money through video teaching, discussions and group activities learn biblical, practical steps for healthy financial stewardship.

  • Cleansing Stream

    Room 103

    Learn how to receive healing and deliverance from your past, so you can walk in the wholeness of Christ and know the freedom He gives. Wherever you are on your journey, this course can help you live your life in Christ to the fullest. Topics include Knowing the Father, Speaking Words of Life, & more. The class culminates in a one-day optional retreat.

  • Rios De Limpieza

    Aprenda a recibir sanidad y liberación de su pasado para que pueda caminar en plenitud de Cristo y conocer la libertad que Él brinda. Donde quiera que esté en su camino, esta clase le puede ayudar a vivir su vida al máximo en Cristo. Temas incluyen: Conocer al Padre, Hablando Palabras de Vida, y otros temas más. La clase culmina con un retiro opcional de un día en un lugar cercano.

  • The Art of Parenting

    Room 101

    In The Art of Parenting, Dennis and Barbara will help you to experience God's truth and apply his Word in your family by focusing your attention on four crucial elements in your children's lives: Identity, Character, Relationships & Mission. When you apply biblical truths in these four areas, you can feel confident your children will have a foundation they can build upon for the rest of their lives.
