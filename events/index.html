The DNA of Cornerstone is to reach as many people for Jesus as we can. Interacting and ministering to the community around us allows us to meet with people where they're at and share the love that Christ gives.

Some of the events that we do

Harvest Fest

With more than 1,500+ attendance from the comunity, enjoy inflatables, food, games, and hear the gospel through presentation. Every October 31st join Cornerstone for a fun-filled evening.

Touch of Christmas

During the Christmas season, touching the community around us has never been more important. Sharing the meaning of Christmas by blessing people with food, coats, drama, and so much more allows us to present Christ to those who may not come to a church service.

5K

Love to run? Join the Cornerstone comunity as we raise money for missions by running a 5K. Not a runner? We need your help. Encourage runners as they cross the finish line!
