Our Events Throughout the Year

Harvest Festival

Every October 31st, Cornerstone hosts an annual Harvest Festival. This outdoor event features a stage presentation by a professional performer and plenty of candy for kids. Please no scary costumes/masks.

A Touch of Christmas

During the Christmas season, touching the community around us has never been more important. Sharing the meaning of Christmas by blessing people with food, coats, drama, and so much more allows us to present Christ to those who may not come to a church service.

Missions Fun Run

Love to run? Join the Cornerstone community as we raise money for missions. Not a runner? We need your help. Participate in the run by serving food, passing out water, and encourage others as they cross the finish line.
