Pastor Mark Lehmann

Lead Pastor

Pastor Mark and his wife, Dee, have been married for 34 years and have two amazing sons, David & Matthew, two wonderful daughters by marriage, Renee & Brandi, and three incredible grandchildren, Evangeline, Bennett & Scarlett. Having served in the Midwest as a lead and staff pastor, they moved to Cornerstone in 1993. Since that time God has been faithful to guide and help them as they lead the body.
