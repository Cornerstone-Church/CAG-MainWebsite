Pastor Mark Lehmann

Lead Pastor

Pastor Mark and his wife, Dee, have been married for 37 years and have two amazing sons, David & Matthew, two wonderful daughters by marriage, Renee & Brandi, and three incredible grandchildren, Evangeline, Bennett & Scarlett. Over the past 25 years that Pastor Mark and Dee have served they have been devoted to building a culture of Prayer, to Reaching our area and our world for Jesus, and to building a church family that reflects heaven, in its unity, diversity and generosity.
In addition to serving as lead Pastor at Cornerstone Pastor Mark Serves as the Assistant Superintendent for the Potomac network and serves on several national boards dealing with missions around the nation and the world.
God has been overwhelmingly faithful in growing Cornerstone Church and Pastor Mark and Dee believe that the best days are still to come!
