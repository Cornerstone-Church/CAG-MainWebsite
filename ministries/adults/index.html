  • A Study of Colossians (Men's Class) - Fireside Room

    George Warren & Darryl Colbert

    This class will study the book of Colossians to help men learn how to apply the principles from the book to their everyday life.

  • Love Your (Muslim) Neighbor - Room 101

    Did you know that Muslims make up about 1 out of every 5 people on earth? Chances are fairly good that you and I share space with Muslims at work, school, or the community. This course will introduce you to the basic teachings of Islam, and offer a biblical model of compassionate ministry to Muslims. The course will glean support from the contemporary experience of a bi-vocational minister who has served over 10 years in the Arab Muslim world, and loves Muslims very much.

  • Look Up, Pay Attention, Expect God (Women's Class) - Room 103

    Lindsay Williams & Nichelle Fullen

    Inspired by great stories of the Bible, this class will explore how prayer and faith affect the life of the believer; how God speaks and how to hear Him; how God’s immutable qualities determine His actions toward His children, when they walk in Him.

  • Marriage Enrichment - Room 105

    Kenneth & Edith Perry

    This class will teach God's purpose and biblical principles for marriage, understanding the covenant made in marriage, and the power of God manifested in yielded vessels; with the goal that God will be glorified, and couples will have a fulfilled and blessed marriage.

  • Introduction to American Sign Language - Room 110

    John Polansky

    Have you always wanted to learn sign language? Or maybe brush up on signs you already know? We would love to have you join us for our fun and interactive class. This class is facilitated in sign language and is offered to hearing, hard of hearing, and Deaf participants.
