Wednesday Service S.O.A.P Social Study

Our heart and passion is for youth-aged students (6th-12th grade) to engage in a life-giving connection with their Creator and be part of a godly community. theLINK is a place where students and leaders can be real, grow, be challenged, worship, give, serve, and simply do life together. At theLINK, we all win when teenagers are connected to Christ and connected to community.


Service Information

Our weekly meetings occur every Wednesday night through Zoom/YouTube (theLINK Online) at 6:45 and in person on the 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 5th Wednesdays of the month from 6:30pm to 9pm. These in-person meetings are broken down by Week 1 (Community Night), Week 2 (HS Night), Week 3 (MS Night), Week 4 (LINK Online) & Week 5 (HS & MS Prayer and Worship Night) of every month.

S.O.A.P. SOCIALS
