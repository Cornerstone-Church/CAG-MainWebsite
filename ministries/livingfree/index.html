Living Free is a discipleship ministry that uses small group based studies, focused on helping us prevent and overcome roadblocks in our faith. The materials are biblically-based, Christ-centered, and designed to benefit all--challenging us to examine our own lives and equipping us to reach our community.

  • Free to Grow

    Free to Grow

    This is a 12-session small group study to help people overcome disappointments and setbacks that have arrested or are presently hindering their emotional and spiritual development.

  • Committed Couples

    Committed Couples

    This 13-session small group study is designed to help each married couple grow spiritually and emotionally in their relationship to each other through an awareness and application of biblical principles.

  • Understanding Depression

    Understanding Depression

    Through this 9-session small group study we learn what depression is, how it affects relationships, and ways to fight depression with Scripture, prayer and mutual support.

  • Grief

    Grief

    This 9-session small group study provides guidance in the use of Scripture to help the grieving. This study provides assistance to those who have suffered any kind of loss (death, divorce, loss of job, etc.)

  • The Single Christian

    The Single Christian

    This 13-week small group study is designed to empower all singles: never married, widowed, divorced, young or older. It also examines the different facets of singleness.

  • The Insight Group

    The Insight Group

    With emphasis on developing Christian character, this 9-session small group study is designed to help individuals overcome a life-controlling problem. This study also serves as the initial training for Living Free facilitators.
