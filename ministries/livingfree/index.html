Living Free is a discipleship ministry that uses small group based studies, focused on helping us prevent and overcome roadblocks in our faith. The materials are biblically-based, Christ-centered, and designed to benefit all. Challenging us to examine our own lives and equipping us to reach our community.

For more information on how to get involved contact connections@cag.org.

  • Authentic Christianity

    Authentic Christianity

    This 6-session study discusses how authentic people who are living in authentic Christian Community are powerful witnesses. This study also deals with what authenticity looks like, the value of authenticity and the marks of authentic Christians.

    Times:
    Thursdays 7:00pm - 8:30pm (9/5 - 10/10)
    Alternating Thursdays 7pm

  • Facing Fear

    Facing Fear

    This is a 10-session curriculum designed to help those controlled by anxiety, fear, and worry. It includes guidance in the use of Scripture to build faith, provides biblical coping methods for facing fear, and is a resource of help for anyone ministering to those who are being controlled or held back by anxiety, fear and worry.

    Time:
    Saturdays 5:30pm - 6:45pm (9/7 - 11/9)

  • Handeling Grief

    Handeling Grief

    This 9-session small group study provides guidance in the use of Scripture to help the grieving. It offers biblical coping methods for facing the loss of a loved-one or a friend and comfort to those who have faced loss. This study primarily provides assistance to those who have suffered loss through death, but also provides help for anyone ministering to those experiencing a "little death" in life: divorce, loss of job, etc.

    Time:
    Thursdays 7:00pm - 8:30pm (9/5 - 11/7)

  • The Single Christian

    The Single Christian

    This 13 week small group study is designed to empower all singles: never married, widowed, divorced, young or older. It also examines the different facets of singleness.

  • Stepping into Freedom

    Stepping into Freedom

    Stepping into Freedom is a 13-week small group curriculum that offers help for anyone struggling with a life-controlling problem like drug addiction, alcoholism, sexual addiction, gambling or workaholism. Through this study, individuals who have a desire to break free from any of these life-controlling problems will learn how to develop roots and steadfastness in Christ to maintain a life of freedom.

    Time:
    Tuesday 7:00pm - 8:30pm (9/3 - 11/26)

  • Understanding Depression

    Understanding Depression

    Through this 9 session small group study we learn what depression is, how it affects relationships, and ways to fight depression with Scripture, prayer and mutual support.

  • Where is the Image of God in You

    Where is the Image of God in You

    The purpose of this 12-session small group study is to help us operate more and more in the image of God according to the way He created us. Through this study we see how some of the different character traits we have can be used in constructive rather than destructive ways in our lives and relationships. Also, this study can help us better understand others as we build godly relationships.

    Time:
    Saturday 5:30pm - 6:45pm (9/7 - 11/23)
