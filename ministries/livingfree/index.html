Living Free

A small group based study, focused on helping us prevent and overcome roadblocks in our faith.

Living Free is a discipleship ministry that uses small group based studies, focused on helping us prevent and overcome roadblocks in our faith. The materials are biblically-based, Christ-centered, and designed to benefit all. Challenging us to examine our own lives and equipping us to reach our community.

For more information on how to get involved contact connections@cag.org.

  • Stepping into Freedom

    Stepping into Freedom (Addiction)

    This small group study offers help for anyone struggling with a life-controlling problem like drug addiction, alcoholism, sexual addiction, gambling or workaholism. Through this study, individuals who have a desire to break free from any of these life-controlling problems will learn how to develop roots and steadfastness in Christ to maintain a life of freedom.

    Time: Mondays | 7:00pm (1/25 - 3/1)

  • Insight: Godly Character (Spanish)

    Insight: Godly Character (Spanish)

    With its emphasis on developing Christian character, this small group study is a powerful tool for those who want to be sure to prevent life-controlling problems from developing in their lives, as well as for those who need to overcome a current problem. This study will enhance people’s understanding of themselves and others.

    Time: Mondays | 7:00pm (1/25 - 3/1)

  • Understanding Depression

    Understanding Depression

    Through this small group study we get an understanding of things such as: what depression is and how it affects relationships; the relationship between depression and spiritual warfare; ways to fight depression with scripture, prayer and mutual support; the special prayer needs of persons with depression; Bible-based coping skills and how to pray for healing and what to expect.

    Time: Mondays | 7:00pm (1/25 - 3/1)

  • Coping with Loss

    Coping with Loss

    Everyone will experience some type of loss during their lifetime. Loss comes in many forms: death, loss of a job, loss of freedom, loss of a relationship and various other types of losses. This small group study covers a multitude of losses that create pain in a person's life. The course goal common to every lesson is to identify troublesome areas of the losses and disappointments of life and to deal with them in constructive ways.

    Time: Mondays | 7:00pm (1/25 - 3/1)

  • Concerned Persons

    Concerned Persons

    This small group study is designed for the many people who have a current or past relationship with a person who has a life-controlling problem. This study emphasizes the need we all have for each other and helps people focus on Christ rather than on the problem.

    Time: Tuesdays | 7:00pm (1/26 - 3/2)

  • Facing Fear (Anxiety)

    Facing Fear (Anxiety)

    This small group study is designed to help those controlled by anxiety, fear, and worry. It includes guidance in the use of Scripture to build faith, provides biblical coping methods for facing fear, provides comfort to those facing fear and is a resource of help for anyone ministering to those who are being controlled or held back by anxiety, fear and worry.

    Time: Tuesdays | 7:30pm (1/26 - 3/2)

  • Forgiveness

    Forgiveness

    This small group study, using the book ‘Bait of Satan’ by John Bevere, is designed to help those dealing with unforgiveness, by identifying areas of offense that “severs relationships and widens the gulfs between us”.

    Time: Tuesdays | 7:30pm (1/26 - 3/2)
