Young Adults Community

Doulos exists to establish an environment where young adults can come unashamed and intimately interact with God. From the very first time you walk through the doors, we hope you feel welcomed and sense community.

Here's what happens on Monday night:

  • Prayer | 6:30 - 7:00pm

    Prayer

    6:30pm - 7:00pm

    Join us as a community to seek the face of Jesus and feel His presence draw near. You can pray for needs in your life, dreams that God has not answered, or even for service. Draw close and experiance God.

  • Dinner | 7:30 - 8:05pm

    Dinner

    7:30 - 8:05pm

    Enjoy felloship with other members of doulos and fill up on a delicious food. Every week a different meal will be served.

  • Worship | 8:05 - 9:30pm

    Service

    8:05 - 9:30pm

    Encounter God and let Him breathe life into your sole. Hear from leaders, teachers, and fellow doulos members on what God has placed on their hearts.
