'' Iron sharpens iron, and one man sharpens another. ''
PRoverbs 27:17 (ESV)

As men, God has called us to both serve and lead in the body of Christ, in our families, at work and in the communities He’s placed us in. God has called you to do things that only you can do – things that matter for his Kingdom. We are seeking to build an authentic community, lift up Jesus, grow in Him and touch our world through a host of opportunities for men to learn, grow, serve and be used for the Kingdom.

Check out these groups you can be apart of:

  • Men's Discipleship Classes

    Men's Discipleship Classes

    Men gather to study God’s Word and discuss their faith together. Topics vary throughout the year. Wednesday nights begin with gathering in the sanctuary for community worship and then we break for classes located in the lower building.

    When: Sundays at 9am & Wednesdays at 7pm (during the school year)

  • Men's Breakfast

    Men's Breakfast

    Come together with other men of God and learn how to lead, grow, and encounter God over breakfast. There will be worship, teaching, and character building activities. When: These occur throughout the year. Check out the events calendar for upcoming breakfasts.

  • Sports

    Sports

    Cornerstone Church and the Men’s Ministry offer various ways to get in the game, literally. As a member of the Bowie Interfaith Recreation Council (BIRC), there are regular opportunities to play volleyball, softball, basketball and other sports with your Christian brothers in a friendly, yet competitive environment.
