    Naomi's Fellowship

    Naomi’s Fellowship is a ministry for widows of the Cornerstone family, as well as for the community. The ladies provide support for each other in prayer, practical help, encouragement, and get together periodically for fun activities such as going to dinner, going to the theatre, etc.

    A Loving Life
