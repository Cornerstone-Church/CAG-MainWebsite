  • Huddleston Life Group

    Huddleston Life Group

    When: 2nd & 4th Monday of the month
    What is the most powerful tool we possess on this Earth? PRAYER! God’s word tells us that the prayers of the righteous avail much. What is the most powerful way to pray? From a position of holiness within the will of God. How do we know? God’s Word is clear. So we long to be powerfully praying women! The people around us deserve that from us. They need our prayers to be effective. So we come together to break open the Word and go deeper into being the women of God that the Lord desires for each of us, purposing to change the atmosphere, and render prayers for our children and others that move the heavens. THERE IS NO ONE LIKE OUR GOD!
