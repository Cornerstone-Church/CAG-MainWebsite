Service Updates

  • December 30, 2021

    Dear Cornerstone Family,

    Greetings in the name of our King Jesus. We are thankful that God’s Word is true. “Do not be afraid or discouraged, for the Lord will personally go ahead of you. He will be with you; he will neither fail you nor abandon you.” Deuteronomy 31:8. Thank God we are never alone, not now, not ever. As we end this year and look toward 2022, we are thankful that this word is true for each of us and we can depend on our Loving God to be with us always.

    As you may know over the last two weeks, many people in our area have tested positive for Covid or have been in contact with someone who has. Because of this we want to be extra cautious to keep others safe, so we are canceling our New Year’s Eve Service scheduled for Friday the 31st. We are asking you to pray for each other and to reach out to your Cornerstone Family and see how they are doing.

    If you would like to give your tithe or offering for 2021, please do so by December 31st at Cag.org/give. Thank you so much for your faithfulness to God, we are touching lives locally and around the world.

    We are praying and believing that the year to come will be one in which we all move closer to Jesus and are “anchored” through prayer, through God’s Word, and in His Presence. We will see you all in person Sunday, January 2 at 9am and 11am or online at live.cag.org.
